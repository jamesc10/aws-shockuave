About

ShockUAVe Systems operated from 2016 through 2018 and served Quarry, Construction, Surveying and GIS Mapping Customers. Utilizing a senseFly eBee RTK mapping drone and a DJI Inspire quadcopter along with Pix4d photogrammetry software, a 3-D model was produced that allowed for detailed data analyis and a high-resoultion othro-mosaic photograph. Horizontal data accuracy was 2-3 cm.

In order to expand the customer base, James attended the New Maps Plus graduate certificate program to learn and deliver interactive maps using the ortho-mosaic photos which enabled the customer to zoom in and make detailed measurement as well as display topography and pile volume data derived from the 3-D model.
